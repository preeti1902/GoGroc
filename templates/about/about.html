#Automate Recurring Orders

GoGroc allows users to set up their grocery orders just once and get the same items delivered automatically every month—no need to reorder manually. Once you place an order and opt for a subscription, it becomes your recurring monthly cart. If you want, you can customize the items before the next delivery; otherwise, the same products will be delivered as scheduled. It's a simple, time-saving way to ensure your essentials never run out.

#Enable Price Comparison

This feature pulls pricing data from major e-commerce platforms—Flipkart, Amazon, and Blinkit—for the same product. It then displays all three prices in one interface so users can make informed decisions on where to buy. Whether someone is budget-conscious or just loves a good deal, this smart comparison tool makes sure they always get the best value.

#Simplify Shopping List Management

Users can upload or manually enter their grocery lists, and GoGroc intelligently maps it to available products for instant checkout readiness.

#Enhance Order Communication & Security

To ensure transparency and easy tracking, each order includes a unique QR code that can be shared via SMS or email. This code acts as a digital receipt and delivery reference, allowing recipients to confirm orders quickly. It's particularly useful for shared households, workplaces, or gifts, where the person receiving the order may not be the one who placed it.
